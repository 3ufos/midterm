APPLE KINGDOM IS UNDER ATTACK

For centuries, applekind enjoyed a peaceful and sweet golden age until suddenly, something began terrorizing the Apple Kingdom. Apples everywhere are turning rotten and being eaten in horrific ways!

There's something sinister going on deep within the core of the Apple Kingdom, and the only hint is a couple of scattered teeth... But who could possibly be brave enough to defeat this evil?

MONSTERS ALL OVER

Sporting names like Rotten To The Core, Peeled Apple, The Molar Terror, Bushel Spoiler, terrifying monsters have appeared all over the Apple Kingdom! Despite some being former apple folk, it seems that they have lost their former identities and now crave apple flesh.

... There are rumors that if you listen carefully to all that groaning, you can hear the name of the one responsible for this mess: "The Doctor"...

AN UNLIKELY HERO!
Bea, a tiny but perfectly ordinary Fuji apple has set out under the King's orders to end the corruption! Wielding nothing but wits and a wodden sword, Bea slices their way to the hearts of the other apple races, and the heart of the Kingdom's deepest secrets.
